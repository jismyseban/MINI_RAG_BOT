Employee FAQ

1. How do I reset my laptop password?
You can raise a ticket in the IT support portal under the category “Login Issues”.
The IT team typically resolves password resets within 2 hours.

2. How can I update my bank account details?
Update your banking details through the HRMS portal under "My Profile → Bank Information".

3. How do I apply for leave?
Leave applications must be submitted in the HRMS portal.
Managers generally approve or reject requests within 24 hours.

4. Who should I contact for payroll issues?
Email: payroll-support@company.com
Response time: 24–48 business hours.

5. What is the reimbursement process?
Submit bills through the Finance portal within 30 days of purchase.
Finance verifies bills and processes reimbursements in the next salary cycle.

6. What is the sick leave Policy?
You can avail unlimited sick leaves during your tenure. 
However you should provide a medical certificate for more than 3 consecutive sick days.

7. Can I get travel reimbursement?
Depending on your project you can avail travel reimbursements upto INR 5000/- every month.
