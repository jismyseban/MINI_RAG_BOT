Company Work Policy - 2024

1. Working Hours
Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are allowed a flexible arrival window between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM.

2. Work From Home (WFH)
Employees may take up to 2 WFH days per week.
WFH requests should be submitted at least one day in advance to the reporting manager.

3. Leave Policy
Employees receive:
- 12 casual leaves per year
- 12 sick leaves per year
- 18 earned leaves per year

Unused earned leaves may be carried forward up to a maximum of 30 days.

4. Dress Code
There is no strict dress code except for client-facing meetings, where business casual is recommended.

5. Laptop & Security
All employees must follow the IT security guidelines:
- No installation of unauthorized software
- VPN must be used when accessing internal systems remotely
- Company data should not be stored on personal devices
