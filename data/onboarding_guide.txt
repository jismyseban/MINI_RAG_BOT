New Employee Onboarding Guide - Quick Reference

1. Day 1 Activities
- Collect laptop from IT desk
- Submit ID verification documents
- Attend orientation session at 11:00 AM
- Set up email and HRMS accounts

2. Tools You Will Use
- Microsoft Teams for meetings
- HRMS Portal for leave and payroll
- Service Desk Portal for IT support
- Confluence for documentation
- Jira for task tracking

3. Mandatory Trainings
New hires must complete mandatory trainings within the first 7 days:
- Code of Conduct
- Cybersecurity Awareness
- Workplace Safety Standards

4. Buddy Program
Each new employee is assigned a buddy for the first 30 days.
Buddies assist with onboarding, day-to-day queries, and team integration.

5. Performance Review Cycle
The company follows a bi-annual performance review cycle in June and December.
Employees set OKRs at the beginning of each cycle.
